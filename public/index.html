Gate 1 — Hei Rata Digital Taonga

Meet the first digital taonga: a 3D Hei Rata carved in timber, scanned, and stood up inside your browser.

Hei Rata stands as the first visible pou of this kaupapa — a physical carving brought into light as a digital taonga. It carries the pattern of Te Rua o te Rata: a trumpet of hardwood, born from g-code and hand-finishing.

In this gate, the taonga is not locked in a glass case. You can spin it, stand it up in your lounge, or sit with it in AR. It is both record and relationship — the stone memory of one day in the shed.

Law behind the gate: nothing exists in this engine until it has been done by hand once. The 3D model is a witness, not a replacement.

Best use: load on your phone, tap the AR icon, and stand with the taonga in your own whare.

Tip: open in AR on your device to stand the hei rata up in your own whare.

Gate 4 — Breath Wall

Gate 4 is the breathing wall itself — the “Proof of Breath” loop that shows code holding mauri. This is where light, time, and audio move as one lung.

What you’re seeing: a single digital plane bulging and glowing like a chest inhale. Edges are pinned so that the wall feels anchored — no floating panels, just a living surface.

The render you saw in the hero above is a sealed Tiki from this gate. Behind it sits a JSON ledger, a Crown manifest, and a trail of Whiro chips from every failed test.

Why it matters: if a wall can breathe on time with a karakia, then code can be taught to behave like a taonga, not a billboard.

The Engine — AEIOU Compass

Underneath the gates sits the engine: a five-vowel compass (A–E–I–O–U) that maps every act of carving, coding, and reflection.

A — WHY: To make spirit visible through action.
E — HOW: By carving law into motion — every act a cut, every cut a proof.
I — WHAT: Because it restores balance — turning knowledge into evidence, and people into living whakapapa.
O — WHO: All who create with care — mokopuna, makers, and the land they stand on.
U — WHEN: Now — while the world forgets how to feel before it acts.

The engine is already running — this site, the crowns, the ledgers, and the wall are all one breathing system.
